## Welcome to HexaCv!

HexaCv is an AI-powered resume builder that makes it easy to create, customize, and export professional resumes. Whether you're starting from scratch or refining an existing resume, HexaCv helps you create a standout CV tailored to any job description.

**Key Benefits:**
- ✅ **Fast:** Build a professional resume in minutes
- ✅ **Smart:** AI-powered alignment with job descriptions
- ✅ **Flexible:** Multiple professional templates to choose from
- ✅ **Accessible:** Works online and offline on any device
- ✅ **Free:** No account required, no hidden fees

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Visit HexaCv

Open your browser and navigate to HexaCv. You'll see the landing page with information about the app and two main options.

### Step 2: Choose Your Path

You have two ways to create your resume:

**Option A: Upload Your Existing Resume**
- Click "Upload Resume" on the landing page
- Select a PDF, Word document, or text file from your computer
- HexaCv will automatically parse and organize your information

**Option B: Build from Scratch**
- Click "Build from Scratch" on the landing page
- Follow the guided form to enter your information section by section
- Takes about 5-10 minutes to complete

### Step 3: Edit and Customize

Once your resume is imported or created, you'll enter the editor where you can:
- Review and edit each section
- Choose from 4 professional templates
- Tailor your resume to a specific job description
- See live updates in the preview

### Step 4: Export and Download

When you're satisfied with your resume:
- Click "Export PDF"
- Your resume will download as a beautifully formatted PDF
- Ready to send to employers!

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Uploading Your Resume

**Supported Formats:**
- PDF (.pdf)
- Word Documents (.docx, .doc)
- Plain Text (.txt)
- Maximum file size: 10MB

**How to Upload:**
1. Click the upload area or drag and drop your file
2. HexaCv will automatically extract and parse your resume
3. Your information will be organized into standard sections
4. Review the parsed content and make any corrections

**What Gets Extracted:**
- **Header:** Name, email, phone, location
- **Summary:** Professional overview
- **Skills:** Technical and soft skills
- **Experience:** Work history with descriptions
- **Projects:** Notable projects and achievements
- **Education:** Degrees and institutions
- **Certifications:** Professional certifications

### Building from Scratch

**Step-by-Step Process:**

**Step 1: Contact Information**
- Enter your full name
- Provide your email address
- Add your phone number
- Enter your location (city, state)

**Step 2: Professional Summary (Optional)**
- Write a brief overview of your professional background
- Highlight your key skills and career goals
- Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
- You can skip this section if you prefer

**Step 3: Skills**
- Add skill categories (e.g., Frontend, Backend, Soft Skills)
- List skills within each category
- Separate multiple skills with commas
- Example: "React, Vue.js, Angular, TypeScript"

**Step 4: Work Experience**
- Enter company name
- Add your job title/role
- Specify start and end dates
- Describe your responsibilities and achievements
- Add multiple entries for different positions

**Step 5: Projects**
- List notable projects you've worked on
- Include project name and description
- Add technologies used
- Include links if applicable

**Step 6: Education**
- Enter institution name (university, college, etc.)
- Specify degree earned (B.S., M.A., etc.)
- Add field of study
- Include graduation date

**Step 7: Certifications**
- List professional certifications
- Include issuing organization
- Add certification date

**Step 8: Review**
- Review all your information
- Make any final edits
- Click "Continue to Editor" when ready

### Using the Resume Editor

**Layout:**
- **Left Panel:** Template and job description selectors
- **Center Panel:** Edit your resume content
- **Right Panel:** Live preview of your resume

**Selecting a Template:**

HexaCv offers 4 professional templates:

1. **Classic ATS Blue**
   - Traditional single-column design
   - Optimized for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
   - Best for: Corporate roles, traditional industries
   - Features: Clean, professional, easy to scan

2. **Minimal Executive**
   - Clean, modern aesthetic
   - Spacious layout with minimal styling
   - Best for: Senior positions, executive roles
   - Features: Elegant, sophisticated, high-impact

3. **Modern Sidebar Lite**
   - Contemporary two-column layout
   - Sidebar with contact info and skills
   - Best for: Creative professionals, tech roles
   - Features: Modern, visually interesting, organized

4. **Technical Compact**
   - Dense, tech-focused design
   - Optimized for technical roles
   - Best for: Developers, engineers, technical professionals
   - Features: Compact, code-friendly, technical aesthetic

**Changing Templates:**
1. Click the template dropdown in the left panel
2. Select a different template
3. Your resume will update instantly in the preview
4. Try different templates to see which works best for you

### Tailoring to a Job Description

**Why Tailor Your Resume?**
- Increase your chances of passing ATS screening
- Align your skills with job requirements
- Show you're a good fit for the specific role
- Improve your interview chances

**How to Tailor:**

1. **Select a Preset Job**
   - Click the job description dropdown
   - Choose from common roles:
     - Full-Stack Developer
     - Frontend Engineer
     - Backend Engineer
     - DevOps Engineer
     - Data Scientist
     - Product Manager
     - UI/UX Designer
     - QA Engineer

2. **Or Enter a Custom Job Description**
   - Paste the job posting text
   - HexaCv will analyze the requirements
   - Suggested keywords will appear

3. **Review Recommendations**
   - See which keywords from the job description match your resume
   - Match score shows alignment percentage
   - Add suggested keywords to your resume if relevant

4. **Edit Your Resume**
   - Update your skills section with job-relevant keywords
   - Reorder experience to highlight relevant roles
   - Emphasize achievements that match job requirements

### Editing Your Resume

**Edit Mode:**
- Click the "Edit" button to enter edit mode
- Make changes to any section
- Changes appear instantly in the preview

**Making Changes:**
1. Click on a section in the left panel
2. Edit the content in the center panel
3. See updates in real-time on the right
4. Click "Save Changes" when done

**Adding/Removing Sections:**
- Use the visibility toggles in the left panel
- Check to show a section
- Uncheck to hide a section
- Reorder sections by dragging

### Exporting Your Resume

**Exporting to PDF:**
1. Review your resume in the preview panel
2. Click "Export PDF" button
3. Your resume will download as a PDF file
4. The PDF is optimized for printing and screen viewing

**File Naming:**
- Your resume will be named "resume.pdf"
- You can rename it after download if desired

**Using Your PDF:**
- Email to employers
- Upload to job application portals
- Print for interviews
- Share via LinkedIn or portfolio

## Tips & Best Practices

### Resume Content

**Professional Summary:**
- Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
- Highlight your unique value proposition
- Tailor it to the job you're applying for
- Use action words and specific achievements

**Skills:**
- Organize by category (Frontend, Backend, Tools, etc.)
- List most relevant skills first
- Include both technical and soft skills
- Be honest about your proficiency level

**Experience:**
- Use action verbs (Developed, Managed, Led, etc.)
- Quantify achievements when possible ("Increased sales by 25%")
- Focus on impact, not just duties
- List most recent experience first

**Education:**
- Include degree, institution, and graduation date
- Add GPA if 3.5 or higher
- Include relevant coursework or honors if space allows

**Certifications:**
- List relevant professional certifications
- Include issuing organization and date
- Prioritize certifications related to your target role

### Template Selection

**For Corporate Roles:**
- Use "Classic ATS Blue"
- Ensures compatibility with ATS systems
- Professional and traditional appearance

**For Executive Positions:**
- Use "Minimal Executive"
- Clean, sophisticated design
- Emphasizes leadership and experience

**For Creative Roles:**
- Use "Modern Sidebar Lite"
- Contemporary, visually interesting
- Shows design sensibility

**For Technical Roles:**
- Use "Technical Compact"
- Tech-focused aesthetic
- Efficient use of space

### Job Targeting

**Before Tailoring:**
- Read the job description carefully
- Identify key skills and requirements
- Note important keywords

**While Tailoring:**
- Add job-relevant keywords to your resume
- Reorder sections to highlight relevant experience
- Emphasize achievements that match job requirements
- Keep your resume honest and accurate

**After Tailoring:**
- Review the final resume
- Ensure it reads naturally (not keyword-stuffed)
- Check for any formatting issues
- Export and submit with confidence

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! HexaCv processes all your data locally on your device. Your information is never sent to our servers. You have complete control over your data.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No! HexaCv works without any account. Just visit the site and start building.

**Q: Can I use HexaCv offline?**
A: Yes! HexaCv is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your device and use it offline. Your resume data is saved locally.

**Q: How do I install HexaCv as an app?**
A: On desktop, click the install button in your browser address bar. On mobile, use "Add to Home Screen" from your browser menu. You can then use HexaCv like a native app.

**Q: What file formats can I upload?**
A: HexaCv supports PDF, Word (.docx), and plain text (.txt) files. Maximum file size is 10MB.

**Q: Can I edit my resume after exporting?**
A: Yes! Your resume is saved in HexaCv. You can continue editing and export multiple times.

**Q: How do I delete my resume?**
A: Your resume data is stored locally on your device. Clear your browser cache or use your browser's storage settings to delete it.

**Q: Can I have multiple resumes?**
A: Currently, HexaCv stores one resume at a time. You can create a new resume by refreshing the page and starting over.

**Q: Does HexaCv work on mobile?**
A: Yes! HexaCv is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts to your screen size.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Visit HexaStack Solutions at https://www.hexastacksolutions.com/ for support and more information.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: My resume didn't parse correctly**
- Solution: Try uploading a different format (PDF to DOCX, etc.)
- Solution: Ensure your resume follows a standard format
- Solution: Try building from scratch instead

**Issue: The preview doesn't match my edits**
- Solution: Click "Save Changes" to apply your edits
- Solution: Refresh the page and try again
- Solution: Check that the section is set to visible

**Issue: PDF export isn't working**
- Solution: Try a different browser
- Solution: Check your browser's download settings
- Solution: Ensure pop-ups aren't blocked

**Issue: My data disappeared**
- Solution: Check if you cleared your browser cache
- Solution: Try a different browser (data is device-specific)
- Solution: Rebuild your resume from scratch

**Issue: The app is running slowly**
- Solution: Close other browser tabs
- Solution: Clear your browser cache
- Solution: Try a different browser or device
